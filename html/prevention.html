How Virus Spread

COVID-19 infection mainly spreads through:

Human Contact

COVID-19 is primarily transmitted through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks, and by close contact with an infected individual.

Air Transmission

It can transmitted through airborne particles, particularly in enclosed spaces with poor ventilation, where the virus can linger in the air and be inhaled by others.

Contaminated Objects

It can be transmitted by touching surfaces or objects contaminated with the virus and then touching the face, particularly the mouth, nose, or eyes.

How To Protect From COVID -19

Vaccination

Get vaccinated as soon as vaccines are available to you. Vaccines have proven to be effective in preventing severe illness and reducing the transmission of the virus.

Mask wearing

Masks help prevent the spread of respiratory droplets that may contain the virus.

Wash Your Hands For 20sec

Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. If soap and water are not available, use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol content.

Cover Nose and Mouth When Sneezing

Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your elbow when coughing or sneezing. Dispose of used tissues immediately and wash your hands afterwards.
